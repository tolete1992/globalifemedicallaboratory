About Us

GLOBALIFE MEDICAL LABORATORY & POLYCLINIC, established in November 2010 with the main branch located at 110 Jasmin Street De Roman Subdivision, Daang Amaya I, Tanza, Cavite. GLOBALIFE MEDICAL LABORATORY & POLYCLINIC, is a DOH (Department of Health) accredited composed of highly trained Medical Technologist with fully automated machines in every section of the Laboratory that will give our patient's/clients an accurate and reliable result. The First Free Standing Private Tertiary Laboratory here in Cavite according to the Department of Health. We have a wide range of quality laboratory services to cater the different laboratory needs of the community.

Our Mission

To improve the health status of the community with a commitment of providing exemplary standards of clinical laboratory practice and effeciency to deliver reliable, cost-effective and exceptional health care service while establishing mutual respect, professionalism, teamwork, motivation and open communication with our employees and clients.

Our Vision

The GlobaLife Vision is to be recognized as one of the nation's best health care institution by providing new clinical and medical innovations, carrying out programs on patient's unique needs, and insuring the optimal level of service.
